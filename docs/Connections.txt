## General

12V supplied via lab power supply
GND LAB connected to GND ESP

### HEATER POWER
12V to mossfet module(HEATER) IN+ 
GND to mossfet module(HEATER) IN-
mossfet module(HEATER) OUT- to Current sensor INA226 I-
Current sensor INA226 I- to Heater
mossfet module(HEATER) OUt+ to Heater
ESP PIN 4 to mossfet module(heater) Signal
GND LAB POWER to mossfet module(heater) Signal

### Fan power
12V to mossfet module(FAN) IN+ 
GND to mossfet module(FAN) IN-
ESP PIN 5 to FAN PWM
ESP PIN 10 TO MOSSFET module(fan) signal
GND LAB POWER TO MOSSFET module(fan) signal

THERMOCOUPLE MAX 6675
GND ESP to GND Thermocouple module
VCC ESP to VCC Thermocouple module
ESP pin 13 to SCK Thermocouple module
ESP pin 12 to CS Thermocouple module
ESP pin 11 to SO Thermocouple module

Powersensor INA226 (I²C address: 0x41)
ESP pin 15 to SDA INA226
ESP pin 16 to SCL INA226
VCC ESP to VCC INA226
GND ESP to GND INA226
ALE INA226 NC
VBS INA226 NC

Pressure Sensor sensirion (I²C address: 0x40)
ESP pin 15 to SDA Pressure sensor
ESP pin 16 to SCL Pressure sensor
VCC ESP to VCC Pressure sensor
GND ESP to GND Pressure sensor

Humidity sensor sensirion (I²C address: 0xF6)
ESP pin 15 to SDA Humidity sensor
ESP pin 16 to SCL Humidity sensor
VCC ESP to VCC Humidity sensor
GND ESP to GND Humidity sensor
